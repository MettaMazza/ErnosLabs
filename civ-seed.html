Preservation

Civ-Seed

A complete, self-contained knowledge archive designed to survive the loss of civilisation — the mathematics, the writing, and the means to rebuild, in one package that depends on no institution and no network. The Smithian Fold is its primary mathematics: a physics anyone can re-derive from a single axiom, carrying zero institutional authority and zero free parameters.

The idea is redundancy as permanence: knowledge that no one owns and no one can quietly delete, because anyone can hold a copy and reseed it. The archive pairs with the Seed Vault — the reader for its library and stone — and with the Library.
