AI, derived not trained

UnisonAI

An AI architecture that is not designed but forced — derived from the fold's own laws, with zero parameters and zero gradient steps. Knowledge is stored as exact held orbits (the tablebase pattern applied to text); attention is unit-capacity selection. Reading its training text once, in 26 seconds, it beat its trained transformer twin on held-out text — 1.289 vs 1.888, the widest margin of the campaign.
