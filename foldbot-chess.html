Zero-parameter engine

FoldBot Chess

A chess engine with zero parameters — no piece values, no tuned weights, no opening book, no training. Every evaluation is a count performed on the board's geometry and an exact rational share of the One. In three days of measured climbing it beat Stockfish's Elo-1900 setting 62.5% and holds Elo-2100 to a draw in half its games.

The same machinery solved queen-vs-rook exactly — 19,733,336 legal positions, re-proven value-by-value in an independent clean-room implementation with zero disagreements — atop a campaign record of over a billion five-piece positions solved at zero error against the Syzygy tablebases. Nothing here is fitted: every release must beat its predecessor head-to-head before it faces Stockfish, and every loss is autopsied under full-strength judgment.
