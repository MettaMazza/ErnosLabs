Zero-parameter engine

FoldBot Chess

A full legal chess engine with zero parameters — no piece values, tuned weights, opening book, or training. Every evaluation is counted from the board and expressed as an exact rational share of the One. It secured Stockfish Elo 1900 at 62.5%; the board below showcases its recorded victory from the current Elo-2100 campaign.

Evaluation without a table of values

The board supplies every number

FoldBot does not begin by declaring a queen worth nine or a rook worth five. Worth changes with position because it is the number of squares a piece can command from where it stands.

CommandCount every square each piece controls

FoldComplement through 1 − theirs at each ply
