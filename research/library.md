## 1. Thesis & what it is
Two linked bodies of writing, ~**1.67 million words**, all *"free to read, free to check"*:
- **The Last Mind** — a six-part work: the origin novel **A Mind Is Born** (Book One) + five volumes that argue four institutions (finance, the state, the church, consensus/knowledge) are **one mechanism of institutional capture** wearing different masks, unified in Volume Five.
- **Owned by No One** — the ErnosDecent book: the *constructive* counterpart — what to build instead, in plain language.

Every work is bylined **Maria Smith** "*with operational assistance from Claude (Anthropic), under the protocol the work itself describes*" — the AI collaboration is disclosed, not hidden. The throughline with the code and the theory is the same ethos: *the work is on disk / on the page; check it yourself.*
